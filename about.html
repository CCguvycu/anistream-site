⟐ What is AnimeWeb?

AnimeWeb is a static, free anime web platform that combines the AniList database with 60+ embeddable streaming sites. It doesn't host any content — it aggregates metadata from AniList and lets you browse directly to streaming sites that don't block embedding.

No sign-up, no subscription, no ads injected by us. Open the site, find something to watch, and hit Browse Free to start streaming.
